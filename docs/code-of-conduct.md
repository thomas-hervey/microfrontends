# Code of Conduct

## Introduction

We, the contributors, maintainers, and participants of this microfrontend application, are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community. We value the contributions of all individuals and aim to provide a safe and respectful environment for everyone, regardless of their background, experience, or identity.

This Code of Conduct outlines our expectations for behavior within our community, as well as the consequences for unacceptable conduct.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants in our community to adhere to the following principles:

1. **Be Respectful:** Treat all individuals with respect and kindness, and consider the impact of your words and actions on others. We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior.

2. **Be Inclusive:** We welcome contributions from people of all backgrounds and identities. Create an environment where everyone feels included and valued.

3. **Be Collaborative:** Work together towards common goals and objectives. Respect differing opinions and seek consensus in decision-making.

4. **Be Open:** Share your knowledge and experience openly. Listen to feedback and be willing to learn from others.

5. **Be Professional:** Conduct yourself professionally and refrain from any behavior that may harm the reputation of the community or its members.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment or discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristics.
- Offensive comments, verbal or written.
- Deliberate intimidation or stalking.
- Disrespectful or disruptive behavior during community events, meetings, or discussions.
- Any form of unwelcome sexual advances or attention.
- Publishing others' private information, such as physical or electronic addresses, without explicit permission.

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers or administrators at [thomasahervey@gmail.com]. All reports will be treated confidentially and will be reviewed and addressed as appropriate.

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in temporary or permanent restrictions, such as warnings, suspension, or removal from the community, at the discretion of the project maintainers.
